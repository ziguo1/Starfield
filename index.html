Deep Space Exploration

In the distant future, you peek into the vastness of space outside of your sleeping quarters, staring into empty space as stars shoot by at warp speed. You fall asleep to the gentle hum of the ship's engines.
"Oddball" classes - nearby moving emissive stars, non-emissive "planets", and stationary background stars
Hold a mouse button to enter hyperwarp speed, and hit the space bar to "pause" the spaceship.
